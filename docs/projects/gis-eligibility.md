## Overview

Applied spatial overlay analysis and satellite imagery interpretation to identify areas suitable for landscape restoration and carbon project interventions, supporting eligibility assessment for restoration programs across Africa.

**Study Area:** Africa<br>
**Duration:** Oct 2025 – Jul 2026<br>
**Role:** Monitoring and Data Program Officer, RMEL Team<br>
**Status:** Completed


---

## Methods & Tools

**Data Sources**

- Satellite imagery (for vegetation/land cover interpretation) <!-- specify sensor, e.g. Sentinel-2, Landsat -->
- Restoration program eligibility criteria (Gold Standard requirements)

**Processing Steps**

1. Interpreted satellite imagery to assess current land cover and vegetation conditions across candidate restoration areas.
2. Performed spatial overlay analysis in ArcGIS Pro, combining land cover, land use, and program eligibility criteria layers.
3. Identified and delineated areas suitable for landscape restoration and carbon project interventions.
4. Cross-checked eligibility outputs against restoration program requirements (e.g. Gold Standard).

**Tools Used**

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| ArcGIS Pro & QGIS | Spatial overlay analysis and eligibility mapping |
| Satellite imagery | Land cover and vegetation condition assessment |
